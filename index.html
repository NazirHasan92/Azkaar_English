Morning and Evening Azkaar (Roman English)

1 After Fajr Prayer

(a) Immediately after Fajr

Without talking to anyone, recite 10 times (also recite in evening). 4th Kalima - 10 good deeds, 10 sins forgiven, 10 ranks elevated, protected from all calamities and Shaitan throughout the day
Laa-ilaaha illallahu wahdahu la-shareekalahu; Lahul mulku wa-lahul h'amdu; Yuh'yee wa-Yumeetu; Wa-huwa a'la kulli shay'in qadeer.

(b) After Fajr, After Maghrib - 7 times

(Mishkaath) - Protection from Fire
Allahumma a'jirni minan naar

(c) After Fajr Prayer - 3 times

Protection from insanity, leprosy, blindness, paralysis
Subh'anallahil Azimi wabih'amdhihi wa La h'aula wala quwwata illa billah

2 Morning & Evening - 7 times

(Tafseer Rooh-ul-Maani) - Allah will remove worldly and hereafter worries
Hasbi-yallahu laa-ilaaha illa huwa aa'layhi tawak-kalthu; Wa-huwa rabbul ar'shil azeem.

5 Morning & Evening Dhikr

Protection from Shaitan from morning till evening, evening till morning. No harm to wealth or home. (Daarmi)
A'uzu billahissamee'il A'leemi minashaitanirrajeem.

Surah Al-Fatiha

Bismillaahir Rahmaa-nir Raheem
Alhamdu lillaahi rabbil aa'lameen; Ar-rahmaanir-raheem; Maaliki'yaw mid-deen; Iyyaa ka na'budu wa-lyyaa ka nasta'een; Ih'dinassiraa'tal mustaqeem; Siraatal-lazeena an'amta alai'him ghayril maghzoo bi'alai'him wa'laz dhaalleen;

Ayatul Kursi

Bismillaahir Rahmaa-nir Raheem.
Alif Laam Meem; Za'likal kitaabu-laa raiba feehi hudal-lil muttaqeen; Al-lazina yu'minoona bil'ghaibi wa-yu'qeemoonas salata wa mim'maa razaqna'hum yun'fiqoon; Wal-lazina yu'minoona bimaa un'zila ilaika wama un'zila min qablika wa-bil aa'khirati hum yu'qinoon; Ulaa-ika a'laa hudammir rabbihim; wa-ulaa-ika humul muf'lihoon;
Allahu la-ilaaha illaa'hu wal haiyul qai-yoom; Laa-ta'khuzuhoo sinatu'oo walaa na'uum; Lahoo ma-fis'samaawaati wama fil ardh; Man zal'lazi yashfa'u in'dahoo illa bi-iz'nihi; Ya-lamu ma-baina ai'deehim wama khal-fahum; Wa-laa yuhee'toona be-shai-im-min il-mihee illa be'maa shaa a; Wasi'aa kursi'yuhus samaa-waati wal ardh; Wa-laa yaoo'duhoo hif'zuhumaa wa-huwal ali'yyul azeem; Laa ik'raaha fid-deeni; Qad-tabay'yanar rushdu minal ghayy; Famai yakfur bit-taa'ghooti wa-yu'mim billaahi faqadis tamsaka bil'urwatil wusqaa lan-fisaama lahaa; Walla'hu samee'un aleem;
Allahu wali-yyul lazina aa'manu yukh'rijuhum minazzulumaati ilan noor; Wal-lazi na-kafaroo awliyaa uo'humut taa'ghootu yukh-rijoo'nahum minan noori ilazzulumaat; Ulaa-ika as'haabun naari hum fee-haa khaa'lidoon.
Bismillaahir Rahmaa-nir Raheem:
Lillaahi maa fis-samaawaati wama fil ardh; Wa in'tubdoo ma-fee an'fusikum aw tukh foohu yu'haa-sibkum bihil-laa; Fa-yagh'firuli maiya-shaa'u wa-yu'azzibu maiya-shaa; Wallahu ala kulee shay'in qadeer.
Aa-manar rasulu bimaa un'zila ilaihi mir-Rabbihee wal-mu'minoon; Kul'lun aa-mana billahi wamalaa-ikathi'hee wakutu-bhi'hee wa-rusu'lihee; Laa-nufar'riqu baina ahadimmir-rusulih; Wa-qaloo sami'naa wa-aata'naa; Ghufra-naka rabbana wa-ilaikal maseer.
Laa yukalliful-laahu nafsan illaa wus'ahaa; Lahaa ma-kasabat wa-aa'laihaa mak-tasabat; Rabbana laa tu'aakhid-naa in'naa-seenaa aw-akhtaa'naa; Rabbana wa-laa tahmil-alainaa isran kamaa hamal'tahoo alal-ladhina min qablinaa; Rabbana wala tu'ham-milna ma-laa-taa qata-lanaa bih; Wa'fu ann'aa; waghfir-lana; war-ham'naa; Antha mawlana fansur-na alal qaw-mil kaa'fireen
